About us

Division of Distance Learning has been created at the Academy of the Ministry of Internal Affairs in 2018. The division provides access to quality education for law enforcement employees and the Academy students.

Creation of this division aims at supporting modern challenges of distance learning, which is focused on training prospective police officers equipped with the relevant skills and knowledge for academic education that will enable them to perform police functions effectively.
